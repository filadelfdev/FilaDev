FAQ

Questions answered

WhatDash works alongside your live streams — you register sales as they happen during your session. We're not a direct platform integration; instead, we give you a fast, frictionless way to log sales and see your real profit in seconds. Think of it as your profit co-pilot during every stream.

Head to the Inventory page and add your products with their cost price, sale price, and stock level. During a live session, you select a product, enter the final sale price and quantity, and WhatDash handles the math. Setup typically takes under 10 minutes.

The moment you register a sale during a live session, your profit, revenue, cost, and margin all update instantly — no refresh needed. This uses Supabase Realtime under the hood, so if you have a team member helping log sales, both of you see the numbers update live.

Each live session is tied to one platform — Whatnot, TikTok Shop, or Amazon Live. If you stream on multiple platforms simultaneously, you can run separate sessions. The Overview dashboard then shows you comparative performance across all platforms.

Yes. The Free plan lets you run 1 active live session, manage up to 100 products, and access the overview dashboard — no credit card required. Upgrade to Pro or Business when you need unlimited sessions or team features.

WhatDash uses Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS), meaning every user can only access their own data or teams they belong to. There is zero cross-tenant access. Stripe handles all billing — we never store card details.
